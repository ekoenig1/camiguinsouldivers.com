Welcome to Camiguin Souldivers

Camiguin Island has some of the best diving sites in the Philippines on pristine reefs by beautiful white sandy beaches. We offer scuba diving and accommodation away from the crowds of the larger resorts at budget prices.

We offer Fun Dives and Training Courses

Introduction to Diving for 12 years old and above. PADI Instructors available. Camiguin’s top dive site Mantigue Island is just 25 minutes away on our dive boat. We do the cheapest 2 dive daytrips to Mantigue. Lots of macro, frogfish, clownfish, scorpionfish and many turtles. We are situated right on the edge of the ocean and have a beautiful house reef 20 metres from shore, ideal for snorkelling.
